Problem Statement

Amani Women Center (AWC) has a mission to empower and educate refugee and immigrant families in the metro
Atlanta community through culturally-tailored programs that contribute to participants to the economic security and over all well being.
The site is unclear about who is served and how the general public can contribute (as volunteers and through financial donations).
Ambiguities cause the organization to miss out on potential donors and volunteers.
We hope to improve engagement with a redesign so that visitors can see the organization serves an important need.
They will fully trust that contributions will be fairly allotted and put to good use, and volunteering will serve a really worthy cause.
